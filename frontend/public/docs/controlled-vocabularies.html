Controlled vocabulary for classifying different types of research outputs and publications:

ID Type Description African Context Examples
1 Dataset Collection of data Climate data, Agricultural surveys, Health statistics
2 Text Textual resources Research papers, Theses, Traditional stories
3 Software Computer programs Agricultural modeling tools, Language processing software
4 Image Visual materials Satellite imagery, Cultural artifacts, Medical scans
5 Sound Audio recordings Oral histories, Traditional music, Language samples
6 Audiovisual Video content Documentary films, Educational videos, Cultural ceremonies
7 Collection Aggregated resources Digital libraries, Museum collections, Archive series
8 Event Documented events Conferences, Workshops, Community gatherings
9 PhysicalObject Physical items Archaeological artifacts, Traditional tools, Specimens
